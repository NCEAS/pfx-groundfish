Functional diversity:
For shallow ecoregions, a 1-way ANOVA indicated significant differences in Rao's Q between ecoregions (p = 0.002), and a Tukey test indicated significant pairwise differences between regions 6 and 9 and each of regions 1, 2, and 4 (9-4 and 9-1 comparisons were marginally significant (p < 0.1); all other comparisons had p < 0.05).
For deep ecoregions, a 1-way ANOVA indicated no significant differences in Rao's Q between ecoregions (p = 0.071).
